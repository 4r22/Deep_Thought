VC Brain

A data- and AI-first operating system for early-stage venture: discover exceptional founders, run honest diligence, and produce a decision-ready memo — fast enough to deploy a $100K check inside 24 hours.

Three layers

Memory

A structured, deduplicated knowledge base of founders, companies, and signals — housing a persistent Founder Score that follows the person across startups and never resets, with an explicit method for pricing thin evidence rather than penalising it.

Intelligence

The reasoning layer. A per-deal forum builds the reference space first; a standing counsel of three offices — Founder, Market, Idea-vs-Market — then cross-examines that record and scores independently. The axes are never averaged into one number: disagreement is signal.

Experience

Investor-grade interface: Notion-approachable, Bloomberg-deep. A run viewer over committed pipeline tapes and a keyboard-driven terminal over the research corpus, both hand-rolled with zero runtime dependencies.
