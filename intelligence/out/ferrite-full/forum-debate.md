## Moderator summary

### Central disagreement

Both debaters accept a modest-truth world is plausible after routed checks: smaller install base than the deck, MRR below $18k, benchmark valid at early scale, real product and real shipping velocity undisputed. They diverge on what the fund's pre-track-record / shipping-velocity thesis actually licenses at fast-check size. Bull treats commercial verification as downstream work the $100K is designed to fund—execution proof plus any real deployment clears the bar. Bear treats load-bearing, subject-sourced commercial claims combined with registry contradiction as a pre-capital integrity test: execution proves the founder can build, but uncorroborated traction narrative fails a hard gate unless reconciled revenue and paying-account thresholds are met first. The disagreement is instrument semantics and thesis interpretation, not whether Ferrite ships code or whether diligence can settle the numbers.

### Position evolution
- bull: **refined, then held**; opened on write-now execution conviction (Turn 1), narrowed to a explicit commercial floor—zero revenue plus no partner attestation = pass (Turn 7), conceded deck scale cannot carry urgency and registry gap is serious (Turn 12), but maintained modest truth plus any real deployment justifies the check (Turns 4, 10, 12).
- bear: **refined, then held**; opened on pass/gate until reconciliation (Turn 2), sharpened gate to ~$12k MRR, two named payers, auditable install definition (Turn 8), conceded real product and technical validation (Turn 13), but maintained uncorroborated commercial claims block write-before-diligence regardless of ship cadence (Turns 5, 11, 13).

### Residual candidates
- **Write on execution with open commercial truth (bull):** Modest reconciled MRR, registry-scale actives, one design-partner-confirmed agent-CI deployment, and sustained ship cadence suffice; fast check buys reconciliation rather than requiring it as precondition.
- **Gate on commercial integrity regardless of ship velocity (bear):** Registry-deck contradiction plus deck-only revenue/load-bearing scale claims require reconciled ~$12k MRR, two named payers, and auditable install definitions before any check; execution alone is pre-traction tolerance, not thesis-faithful pre-seed posture.
